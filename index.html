Performance, Goals & Rewards

Internship Nature: This is a conditional, unpaid internship with a performance-based opportunity for future hiring. The intern must achieve a minimum of 5 confirmed business deals during this period to be considered for a permanent or extended position.

Commission Policy: Interns will be entitled to commission for each successful deal even before being formally hired. Commission payments will be made after verification and closure of each deal, based on agreed terms. This does not guarantee employment but rewards performance during the internship period.

Performance & Conduct Goal: A minimum of 5 business deals must be completed by August 22, 2025.

Professional Conduct & Company Policies

Confidentiality & Non-Disclosure: The intern shall not discuss or leak any internal discussions, communications, strategies, or client information, during or after the internship. Breach of confidentiality will result in immediate termination and may lead to legal consequences.

Intellectual Property: All data, contacts, materials, content, and strategies created or accessed during the internship are the sole property of Shayan Irfan. The intern shall not reuse, distribute, or claim ownership over any such material after departure.

General Conduct: The intern must maintain professional conduct, daily check-ins, and responsiveness. Failure to perform or inappropriate behavior may result in immediate dismissal.

Legal Terms & Conditions

Termination Clause: This contract can be terminated without notice if the intern violates any confidentiality terms, shows negligence, misconduct, or fails to perform, or engages in any behavior that negatively impacts the company.

No Employment Guarantee: This agreement does not guarantee permanent hiring. Selection for employment depends on final evaluation and performance-based eligibility.

Parties to the Agreement: This Internship Agreement ("Agreement") is made and entered into on this 22nd day of July, 2025, by and between Shayan Irfan THE MASTERMINDS, and the Intern.
